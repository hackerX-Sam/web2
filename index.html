ABOUT THE EVENT

WHAT IS CODE नक्षत्र II?

CODE नक्षत्र II is a cutting-edge hybrid Technical Community constructed by the ambitious students of This event is designed to bridge the gap between creative brainstorming and technical implementation, fostering a space where innovation meets practical solutions.

The journey begins with a dynamic Online Round where participants present their visionary projects to a panel of expert mentors. This serves as a filter for the grand finale: a high-stakes Offline Round. Here, the brightest minds compete for recognition, networking with peers, and potentially winning prizes for their groundbreaking work.

Still confused about the format? Feel free to contact us anytime for more details.

What is Code Rangers?

Code Rangers is the premier technical community of Trinity Institute of Innovation in Professional Studies We are dedicated to nurturing the next generation of technologists through collaboration, innovation, and hands-on experience.

From organizing national-level hackathons like CODE नक्षत्र II to hosting technical workshops and building impactful projects, Code Rangers provides a platform for students to grow their technical prowess and connect with industry leaders.

FAQs
FAQs
Team Size

4 members

Registration Costs?

Nada. (It’s free!)

Are inter-college teams allowed?

Yes, inter-college teams are allowed.

What’s the duration of the hackathon?

It’s a 24-hour offline hackathon (be prepared to hack all night!).

What should I bring offline to the hackathon?

Bring your laptop, charger, extension board, any necessary software/tools and a winner mindset.

I’m a beginner. Should I apply?

Yes! Beginners are absolutely welcome. Hackathons are great for learning, teamwork and hands-on experience in a fun, supportive environment. Jump in and grow.

Do I need to bring food or snacks?

Nope! We’ve got all the snacks and caffeine you need just bring your ideas and an empty stomach!

Is this hackathon open for all domains?

Yes, it’s open to all domains tech, design, business. Bring your skills and let’s build!

Will travel expenses be covered?

No, we’re unable to cover travel expenses.

What is the theme of the hackathon?

No theme. No limits. Build something innovative, creative, or even crazy just make it impactful.

When will teams receive the acceptance email?

Acceptance emails will be rolled out in April. Please keep an eye on your registered email for updates.

Are School students Allowed too?

Yes, we welcome passionate school students to participate and showcase their potential!
