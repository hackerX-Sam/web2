ABOUT THE EVENT

WHAT IS CODE नक्षत्र II?

CODE नक्षत्र II is a cutting-edge hybrid Technical Community constructed by the ambitious students of TIIPS, Greater Noida. This event is designed to bridge the gap between creative brainstorming and technical implementation, fostering a space where innovation meets practical solutions.

The journey begins with a dynamic Online Round where participants present their visionary projects to a panel of expert mentors. This serves as a filter for the grand finale: a high-stakes Offline Round hosted at TIIPS, Knowledge Park 2. Here, the brightest minds compete for recognition, networking with peers, and potentially winning prizes for their groundbreaking work.

Still confused about the format? Feel free to contact us anytime for more details.

What is Code Rangers?

Code Rangers is the premier technical community of Trinity Institute of Innovation in Professional Studies (TIIPS). We are dedicated to nurturing the next generation of technologists through collaboration, innovation, and hands-on experience.

From organizing national-level hackathons like CODE नक्षत्र II to hosting technical workshops and building impactful projects, Code Rangers provides a platform for students to grow their technical prowess and connect with industry leaders.

FAQs

FAQs

When and where will "CODE नक्षत्र II" take place?

The online round begins in early September, while the grand offline finale takes place on September 25-26, 2024, at the TIIPS campus in Greater Noida.

Who can participate in "CODE नक्षत्र II"?

All students and tech enthusiasts from across the nation are welcome! Whether you're a beginner or an expert, if you love building things, this is for you.

How can I register and is there any fee?

Registration is completely FREE! You can sign up through the "Register Now" button at the top of our page via the Devfolio/Namespace link.

What benefits will participants get?

Aside from the massive 1,00,000+ prize pool, participants get official certificates, exclusive swags, professional mentorship, and networking opportunities with industry leaders.

Can I participate alone or do I need a team?

You can participate in teams of 1 to 4 members. If you don't have a team, don't worry! We have networking sessions to help you find like-minded teammates.

What should I bring to the event?

Bring your laptop, chargers, any specific hardware you need, and a whole lot of energy! We also recommend bringing personal toiletries for the overnight stay.

Is there any travel reimbursement?

Currently, we do not provide travel reimbursement, but we ensure top-notch hospitality, food, and mentorship for all 24-hour finalists at our venue.

Will food and beverages be provided?

Absolutely! We provide full meals, snacks, and plenty of caffeine during the offline round to keep your brain firing at 100%.

Can I attend the hackathon remotely?

The initial screening round is online, but the final 24-hour hackathon is an in-person event to ensure the best collaborative experience.

Number of hours hackathon is going to last?

The main offline hackathon is a continuous 24-hour coding sprint. Get ready for an intense day and night of innovation!

Will there be internet access available?

Yes, TIIPS provides high-speed Wi-Fi infrastructure for all participants. We recommend having your own backup hotspot just in case!

Is there any accommodation facility?

Yes, we provide dedicated resting zones and basic facilities for participants to stay overnight comfortably at the venue.

Can I start working on my project before the hackathon?

No, all code must be written during the 24-hour event. You can, however, brainstorm ideas, design wireframes, and choose your tech stack in advance!

What are the judging criteria?

Our judges evaluate projects based on Innovation, Technical Complexity, Practical Utility, and the Quality of the final pitch. Show us something stellar!
